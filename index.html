Hello and welcome to my home page! This is a space where I can document my journey toward becoming a web developer!

I'm originally from Muriwai, North-West of Auckland, and now reside in Kingsland, Auckland City. I have a Science Bachelors Degree majoring in Chemistry, and had been working in product development for two years before making the leap to web development here at Dev Academy. I'm making this leap because I decided to take control of my future and happiness and enter a field where I can take advantage of both my love for problem solving and my love for working in a fun, fast paced, innovative environment.
